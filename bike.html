Bike

I got an old road racing bike, and transformed it in a nice road commuter bike.

First I dismantled everything and cleaned all the parts. After sanding the frame a base layer was applied followed by three layers of paint with the name "Strong Ocean". The paint job was finalized with several layers of clear coat. Some parts where switched for orange ones to give a nice look. The handlebars where changed to touring handlebars and a modern stem was installed. This gives the rider a more upright position and makes the bike more comortable to ride. The tires where switched for puncture resistant tires in the widest size the frame could fit. This improves riding comfort and keeps maintenance low.
